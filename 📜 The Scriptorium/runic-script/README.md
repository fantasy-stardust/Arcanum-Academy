# runic-script | 语文

The first rune any student learns is the language already living in the mouth and memory.

每位学徒最先学会的符文，其实就是早已栖居在口中与记忆里的母语。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Runic Script is the study of native language, literature, reading, rhetoric, and written expression. In this hall, texts are not treated as dead pages but as vessels of voice, history, emotion, and form.

语文之学关注母语、文学、阅读、修辞与书面表达。在这座分殿中，文本并不是静止的纸页，而是承载声音、历史、情感与结构的器皿。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Classical and modern texts 经典与现代文本
- Reading response and annotation 阅读理解与批注
- Composition, argument, and narrative 写作、论证与叙事
- Recitation, rhetoric, and language sense 诵读、修辞与语感

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Lesson notes 课堂笔记
- Reading journals 阅读札记
- Essay drafts and revisions 作文草稿与修改记录
- Excerpt collections 摘抄与句段收藏
- Exam review outlines 复习提纲

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

Read until the text begins to read you.

读到最后，愿你也被文字反过来照见。

# arithmancy | 数学

In this chamber, number is treated not as quantity alone, but as a grammar of order.

在这座分殿中，数字不只是数量，更是一种描述秩序的语法。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Arithmancy is the art of structure, proof, relation, abstraction, and disciplined problem solving. Here, certainty is earned step by step, and beauty often appears as precision.

数学之学关注结构、证明、关系、抽象与严谨解题。在这里，确定性需要一步一步地获得，而美感往往以精确的形式出现。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Algebra and functions 代数与函数
- Geometry and spatial reasoning 几何与空间思维
- Probability, statistics, and patterns 概率、统计与规律
- Proof, derivation, and problem strategies 证明、推导与解题策略

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Formula summaries 公式整理
- Solved examples 例题与详解
- Mistake logs 错题记录
- Theorem notes 定理笔记
- Practice sets and review sheets 练习集与复习单

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

What is proven becomes a lantern no doubt can easily extinguish.

凡被证明之物，便成为怀疑也难以吹灭的灯。

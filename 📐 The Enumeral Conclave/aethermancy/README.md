# aethermancy | 物理

Here the world is studied as motion, force, field, and law written across matter.

这里将世界视作运动、力、场与法则在物质上的书写。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Aethermancy concerns the principles that govern motion, energy, waves, electricity, and the structure of the physical world. It asks not only what happens, but why it must happen so.

物理之学关注运动、能量、波、电以及物理世界的运行规律。它追问的不只是“发生了什么”，更是“为什么必然如此”。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Mechanics and motion 力学与运动
- Energy, work, and power 能量、功与功率
- Waves, light, and sound 波、光与声
- Electricity, magnetism, and modern physics 电磁与现代物理基础

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Concept summaries 概念整理
- Formula derivations 公式推导
- Experiment notes 实验记录
- Diagram analyses 图像分析
- Problem sets 习题训练

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

To understand a force is to watch the invisible leave a visible mark.

理解一种力，就是看见无形之物留下有形的痕迹。

# Circuit to Mechanism | 电路到机构

When signal crosses into mechanism, it must become controllable force rather than vague activation.

当信号跨入机构层时，它必须化为可控制的力量，而不是模糊的“被激活”。

---

- Declare what the signal is expected to move or release 说明信号预期要驱动或释放什么
- Record the motion range and stopping condition 记录运动范围与停止条件
- Check whether the driven part returns safely after action 检查受驱部件动作后是否能安全回位
- Note where excess force could damage alignment or joints 标明多余力量可能损伤对位或连接的部位

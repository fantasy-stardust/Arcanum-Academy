# Lessons Learned | 教训摘录

These lessons are kept in short form so they can be consulted before a construct enters proof.

这些教训以短式保存，以便在构造进入受试之前就能被及时查阅。

---

- A clear failure seen early is cheaper than a hidden one discovered late. 越早看见的清楚失败，代价越低于后期才暴露的隐性失效。
- Measurements should be written where others can repeat them, not remembered where only one apprentice can find them. 测量应写在他人可复现之处，而不应只留在某个学徒的记忆里。
- The point where one hall hands work to another is the point most worthy of suspicion. 一个分殿将成果交给另一个分殿的地方，往往最值得优先怀疑。
- A construct that barely works once has not yet earned the right to be called reliable. 只能勉强成功一次的构造，还没有资格被称作可靠。

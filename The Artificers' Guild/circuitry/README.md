# circuitry | 电子

In this chamber, signal and current are taught to move intention through copper, crystal, and measured path.

这里研习的是让信号与电流沿着铜线、晶核与精确路径传递意志的工艺。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Circuitry studies components, circuits, sensors, control, and the ordered flow of power and signal. It teaches apprentices to read diagrams carefully, protect delicate systems, trace faults without haste, and make the unseen behavior of a device observable.

电子之学关注元件、电路、传感、控制，以及电能与信号的有序流动。它要求学徒学会谨慎读图、保护精密系统、耐心追踪故障，并让装置内部那些看不见的行为变得可被观察。

## Hall Emblem | 分殿徽记

The emblem of circuitry is a copper ring enclosing a crystal spark, crossed by a forked trace of light. The ring signifies continuity, the spark signifies charged potential, and the trace signifies the chosen path by which signal becomes command.

circuitry 的分殿徽记被设定为一枚包围晶火的铜环，其上交错着一道分岔的光痕。铜环象征回路的连续，晶火象征蓄积的势能，而光痕象征信号化为指令时所选择的路径。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Components, symbols, and circuit reading 元件识别、符号系统与电路读图
- Analog and digital basics 模拟与数字基础
- Sensors, control, and embedded interaction 传感、控制与嵌入式交互
- Wiring, soldering, testing, and fault tracing 接线、焊接、测试与故障排查

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Circuit diagrams and annotated schematics 电路图与标注原理图
- Component notes and pin references 元件笔记与引脚参考
- Breadboard logs and test records 面包板实验记录与测试日志
- Fault cases and repair notes 故障案例与维修笔记
- Small device projects and control experiments 小型装置项目与控制实验

## Required Tools | 常用器具

- Breadboards, jumper wires, and practice components 面包板、跳线与基础练习元件
- A multimeter for measurement and fault tracing 用于测量与排错的万用表
- A soldering iron and basic soldering supplies 焊接台与基础焊接耗材
- Power sources or battery packs for small circuits 适用于小型电路的供电装置或电池组
- Safety tools such as insulated clips and eye protection 绝缘夹具、护目器等安全器具

## Suggested Projects | 建议项目

- A traffic-light controller 红绿灯控制器
- A light-sensitive alarm or night lamp 光敏警报器或夜灯
- A temperature and humidity monitor 温湿度监测器
- A simple sensor-driven door signal 简易传感门禁提示装置
- A breadboard-based digital logic exercise board 面包板数字逻辑练习板

## Study Path | 学习路径

Apprentices in this hall usually begin by learning to recognize parts and read paths, then move from safe prototyping toward stable control and device integration.

本殿学徒通常先学习辨认元件与读懂路径，再从安全的原型搭建逐步走向稳定控制与装置整合。

1. Recognize common components, symbols, and safe handling rules 识别常见元件、符号系统与安全操作规则
2. Read simple schematics and build basic breadboard circuits 阅读基础原理图并搭建简单面包板电路
3. Study analog and digital behavior through measurement and comparison 通过测量与对照学习模拟与数字行为
4. Add sensors, switches, and control modules to create responsive devices 引入传感器、开关与控制模块，构成可响应装置
5. Advance to fault tracing, signal stability, and integrated small-system design 进阶到故障排查、信号稳定性与小型系统整合设计

## Hall Rite | 分殿仪式

Before a circuit is judged fit to leave the hall, apprentices perform the Rite of the Living Trace. In the Signal Rooms they follow the intended path of current and command, at the Soldering Tables they bind the chosen components into a stable body, and only afterward do they send the device onward to the Proving Chambers.

任何电路若想被认定为可以离开本殿，学徒都必须完成“活迹之仪”。他们先在回路室中追踪电流与指令应当经过的路径，再于焊接台前将选定元件结合成稳定之体，之后才会把装置送往试验间。

Its customary sequence is threefold:

其传统步骤共分三段：

- Read the path 读通路径
- Bind the circuit 结合回路
- Prove the signal 验明信号

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

What travels unseen must still leave a trace fit to read.

凡于无形中流动之物，也必须留下可供阅读的痕迹。

# Safety Discipline | 安全守则

The guild permits difficult work, not careless work.

公会允许艰深的工作，却不宽恕轻率的工作。

---

## Discipline Charge | 守则之责

This scroll is consulted before a tool is powered, before a frame is loaded, and before a construct is trusted near another apprentice.

此卷应在器具通电之前、构架受载之前，以及构造被允许靠近其他学徒之前先被查阅。

## Bench Rules | 工位通则

1. Declare heat, stored force, live signal, and moving mass before work begins. 开工之前，先声明热源、蓄力、带电信号与运动质量。
2. No apprentice works alone on an energized, heated, or spring-loaded construct. 对通电、加热或带弹性预载的构造，不得单独操作。
3. Isolate power, unload tension, and block motion before adjustment. 调整之前，必须先断电、卸载张力并阻止运动。
4. Wear the protection required by the bench, not merely the protection that feels convenient. 护具应按工位要求佩戴，而不可只图方便。
5. A near-failure is recorded before work resumes. 任何险些失效之事，都要先记录，再继续工作。
6. Borrowed tools remain bound by the same caution described in [tool-borrowing-rules.md](tool-borrowing-rules.md). 借来的器具同样受 [tool-borrowing-rules.md](tool-borrowing-rules.md) 约束，不因归属不同而放宽。

## Hall Hazards | 分殿常见风险

- golemcraft: unattended loops, overheated boards, misread state reports 信息技术分殿：无人看守的循环、过热的板件、被误读的状态回报
- circuitry: charged components, exposed conductors, hot solder points 电子分殿：带电元件、裸露导体、灼热焊点
- mechanica: pinch points, sprung members, falling loads, tool kickback 机械分殿：夹点、弹性构件、坠落载荷、器具反冲

## Stop Conditions | 立即停工条件

Work stops at once when any of the following is seen:

一旦出现下列任一情况，必须立即停工：

- Heat, smell, or smoke without an expected source 无法解释的发热、焦味或烟气
- Motion that continues after the stop condition should have been reached 已达停止条件后仍持续的运动
- Signal values that no hall can explain 任何分殿都无法解释的信号值
- New cracking, warping, looseness, or repeated impact 新出现的裂纹、变形、松动或重复撞击

## Return-to-Work Record | 复工前记录

Before the bench is reopened, write the following:

在工位重新开放之前，应写明以下各项：

- First visible symptom 首发症状
- Isolated source or suspected handoff point 已隔离来源或可疑交接点
- What was powered down, unloaded, or blocked 已断开的电源、卸除的载荷或阻止的运动
- Who judged the bench safe to reopen 由谁裁定工位可以复工

## Guild Saying | 公会短谚

The construct that survives haste is rare; the apprentice who relies on that rarity should not remain long at the bench.

能在轻率中幸存的构造极少；若有学徒把希望寄托在这份侥幸上，他也不该久留工位。

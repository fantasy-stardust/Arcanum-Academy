# Lingua-Arcana | 英语

This is the hall of the farther tongue: a language that opens roads beyond one's first horizon.

这里研习的是“更远之舌”之学，一种能把视野带向第一道地平线之外的语言。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Lingua-Arcana studies English as a bridge to wider worlds of reading, listening, speaking, and writing. Its craft is not imitation alone, but the patient learning of another rhythm of thought.

英语之学被视为通向更广阔世界的桥梁，涵盖阅读、听力、口语与写作。它并不仅仅是模仿，更是耐心学习另一种思维节律的过程。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Vocabulary and grammar 词汇与语法
- Listening and speaking practice 听说训练
- Reading passages and close analysis 阅读篇章与精读
- Translation and composition 翻译与写作

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Vocabulary lists 词汇清单
- Grammar summaries 语法整理
- Reading notes 阅读笔记
- Translation exercises 翻译练习
- Speaking prompts and writing drafts 口语题与写作草稿

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

Learn a new tongue, and you inherit a second weather of thought.

多学会一种语言，便像多拥有一层思想的天气。

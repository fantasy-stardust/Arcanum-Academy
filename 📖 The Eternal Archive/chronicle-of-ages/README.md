# chronicle-of-ages | 历史

This hall keeps the memory of peoples, states, conflict, reform, and the long turning of eras.

这座分殿保存族群、国家、战争、改革与时代更替的记忆。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Chronicle of Ages studies civilizations, events, systems, and the continuity of time. It asks apprentices to read the past carefully enough that the present becomes less shallow.

历史之学研究文明、事件、制度与时间脉络。它要求学徒细读过去，直到当下不再显得浅薄。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Historical periods and timelines 历史时期与时间线
- Causes and consequences of events 事件的原因与影响
- Institutions, reforms, and social change 制度、改革与社会变迁
- Source comparison and historical interpretation 史料比较与历史解释

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Timeline notes 时间轴笔记
- Event summaries 事件整理
- Source excerpts 史料摘录
- Essay plans 论述题提纲
- Comparison charts 对比图表

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

The past does not sleep; it continues speaking through what the present repeats.

过去从未沉睡，它只是借由当下的重复继续说话。

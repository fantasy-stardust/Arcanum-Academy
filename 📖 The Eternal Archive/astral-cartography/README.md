# astral-cartography | 地理

In this vault, land, climate, settlement, and movement are mapped as one woven system.

在这座制图密库中，地貌、气候、聚落与流动被视作同一张交织网络。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Astral Cartography studies place, region, climate, environment, and the relationship between people and the earth. It trains apprentices to read both map and landscape as living evidence.

地理之学研究空间、区域、气候、环境以及人与地球之间的关系。它训练学徒同时读懂地图与地表，将二者都视作活的证据。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Earth systems and landforms 地球系统与地貌
- Weather, climate, and circulation 天气、气候与环流
- Population, resources, and settlement 人口、资源与聚落
- Regional geography and map analysis 区域地理与地图分析

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Map annotations 地图批注
- Climate diagrams 气候图整理
- Regional summaries 区域笔记
- Case studies 案例分析
- Human-earth relation charts 人地关系图表

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

To map a land well is to admit that people are never separate from place.

真正绘好一片土地，意味着承认人从未脱离过所居之地。

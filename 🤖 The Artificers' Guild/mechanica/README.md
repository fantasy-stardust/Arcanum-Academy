# mechanica | 机械

Here, force is taught to travel with purpose through frame, linkage, axle, and turning weight.

这里研习的是让力量沿着骨架、连杆、转轴与配重有目的地传递的构造之术。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Mechanica studies structure, motion, transmission, materials, and the discipline of assembly. It teaches apprentices to understand how parts bear load, how motion is guided, how tolerance shapes reliability, and how a mechanism earns trust through fit and endurance.

机械之学关注结构、运动、传动、材料与装配秩序。它要求学徒理解构件如何承载、运动如何被引导、公差如何影响可靠性，以及一套机构如何凭借契合与耐久赢得信赖。

## Hall Emblem | 分殿徽记

The emblem of mechanica is a paired gear and balance-arm set over an iron frame. The gears signify transmission and coordination, the balance-arm signifies measured force, and the frame signifies the discipline that keeps motion from falling into waste.

mechanica 的分殿徽记被设定为一组并列齿轮与一支平衡臂，整体安置在铁制框架之上。齿轮象征传动与协作，平衡臂象征被度量的力量，而外框则象征防止运动沦为耗散的构造秩序。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Simple machines and force analysis 简单机械与受力分析
- Linkages, gears, and motion transmission 连杆、齿轮与运动传递
- Structures, materials, and tolerances 结构、材料与公差
- Assembly drawing, maintenance, and mechanism design 装配图、维护与机构设计

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Exploded sketches and assembly drawings 爆炸图与装配图
- Mechanism notes and force analyses 机构笔记与受力分析
- Material comparisons and tolerance records 材料对照与公差记录
- Build logs and maintenance journals 制作日志与维护记录
- Model prototypes and motion studies 模型原型与运动研究

## Hall Rite | 分殿仪式

Before a mechanism is entrusted with load or motion, apprentices undertake the Rite of True Fitting. At the Engine Bays they study how force will enter and travel, at the Assembly Frames they fit each part until motion runs without strain, and only then is the construct permitted to face the trials of the Proving Chambers.

任何机构若要被托付以承载或运动之责，学徒都必须完成“正配之仪”。他们先在锻机工位前判断力量将从何处进入、沿何处传递，再于装配架旁逐一调合构件，直到运动不再带着勉强与偏斜，之后作品才被允许前往试验间受试。

Its customary sequence is threefold:

其传统步骤共分三段：

- Weigh the force 度量受力
- Fit the frame 校正构架
- Test the motion 验证运动

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

A true machine wastes neither force nor intention.

真正的机械，既不浪费力量，也不浪费意图。

# Apprentice Checklist | 学徒准备清单

This checklist is used before an apprentice joins a cross-hall construct.

这份清单用于确认学徒是否已经准备好参与跨分殿联合构造。

---

## Readiness Check | 准备检查

- Can describe their own hall's contribution in one clear sentence 能用一句清楚的话说明本分殿贡献
- Can read a shared construct name and identify the halls involved 能读懂联合构造命名并识别涉及分殿
- Can record a revision without omitting purpose or risk 能在记录修订时不遗漏用途与风险
- Can explain at least one likely point of failure 能解释至少一个可能的失效点
- Can enter the Proving Chambers with a complete handoff note 能带着完整交接说明进入试验间

## Minimum Conduct | 最低要求

An apprentice who cannot yet record clearly may still practice within a hall, but should not lead an integrated build.

尚不能清楚记录的学徒仍可在分殿内部练习，但不应主持联合造物。

# joint-constructs | 联合造物

This is the workyard where no single hall is sufficient on its own.

这里是单凭任一分殿都不足以独立完成之物的造作场。

---

## Yard Charge | 造物之责

Joint Constructs houses cross-hall builds, templates, and integration notes. It exists so that apprentices do not have to invent collaboration from nothing each time code, signal, and mechanism must move as one.

联合造物层收纳跨分殿构造、项目模板与整合说明。它存在的意义，是让学徒不必在每一次需要代码、信号与机构协同运作时都从零发明协作方式。

What one hall begins in elegance, three halls must finish in agreement.

一殿以优雅开启之物，须由三殿在一致中完成。

# proving-chambers | 试验间

This is where claims of function are asked to endure weight, error, and repetition.

这里是让“它能运行”的宣称真正承受重量、差错与重复检验之处。

---

## Chamber Charge | 试验之责

The Proving Chambers define what must be tested, what must be recorded, and what may enter the guild record as trusted work. They do not exist to punish failure, but to make failure speak clearly.

试验间负责界定何物必须受试、何物必须记录，以及何物才有资格作为可信作品被收入公会档案。它的存在不是为了惩罚失败，而是为了让失败开口说话。

## Chamber Saying | 试验短谚

What survives proof may speak with less pride and more authority.

凡经得起验证之物，说话时便可少一些傲气，多一些凭据。

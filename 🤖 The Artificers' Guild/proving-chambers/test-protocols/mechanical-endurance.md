# Mechanical Endurance | 机械耐受验证

This protocol applies to constructs whose burden lies in structure, load, motion, transmission, or repeated use.

本规程适用于负担主要落在结构、承载、运动、传动或重复使用上的构造物。

---

## Minimum Proof | 最低验证

1. Load enters where the design says it should 受力能从设计规定的位置进入
2. Motion follows the intended path without forced strain 运动沿预期路径进行且无强迫受力
3. Fasteners and joints remain secure after repeated use 紧固点与连接点在重复使用后仍保持稳固
4. Wear points can be identified before failure 磨损点能在失效前被识别
5. Maintenance steps are clear enough for repeat service 维护步骤足够清楚，可重复执行

## Failure Markers | 失败标记

- Misalignment under normal motion 正常运动时出现偏斜
- Rapid wear at undocumented contact points 未记录接触点出现快速磨损
- Loosened joints after short operation 短时运行后连接松脱
- Load shifting into unintended members 受力转移至非预期构件

## Pass Condition | 通过条件

The mechanism may proceed only when it can carry motion repeatedly without hiding strain in silence.

只有当机构能够重复承担运动而不在沉默中隐藏应力时，它才可通过。

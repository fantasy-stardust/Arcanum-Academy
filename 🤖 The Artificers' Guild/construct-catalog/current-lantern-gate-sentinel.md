# Lantern Gate Sentinel | 灯门守望构造

## Record State | 入档状态

`current`

## Hall Configuration | 分殿组合

golemcraft + circuitry + mechanica

## Function Domain | 功能类型

Sensing, timed response, raised gate motion, and stable return.

传感、定时响应、门体抬升运动与稳定回位。

## Why It Is Typical | 何以典型

This construct is the clearest current example of how the three halls reduce their own excess until one stable behavior remains.

此构造是当前最清楚的一例：三座分殿各自削减自身多余之处，直到只剩下一种稳定行为。

## Lessons Kept | 留下的教训

- A narrower pulse may be wiser than a stronger one. 更窄的脉冲，往往比更强的脉冲更有智慧。
- Return balance is part of control, not an afterthought after motion begins. 回位平衡属于控制本身，而不是运动开始后的补救。
- A state threshold becomes trustworthy only when another hall can observe it. 状态阈值只有在另一殿也能观测时，才算可信。

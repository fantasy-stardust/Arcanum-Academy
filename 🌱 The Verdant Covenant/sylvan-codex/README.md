# sylvan-codex | 生物

In this living archive, every cell, leaf, and creature is treated as a script of survival.

在这座活体档案馆中，每一个细胞、叶片与生灵都被视作一段关于生存的文字。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Sylvan Codex is the study of organisms, heredity, ecology, systems of life, and the delicate balance between structure and adaptation. It teaches that living things must be observed with patience rather than haste.

生物之学关注个体、生理、遗传、生态与生命系统的相互平衡。它提醒学徒，生命应当被耐心观察，而不是被急躁裁断。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Cell structure and metabolism 细胞结构与代谢
- Human and organism systems 人体与生物系统
- Genetics and inheritance 遗传与变异
- Ecology and environmental relations 生态与环境关系

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Observation notes 观察记录
- Diagram labels 结构图标注
- System summaries 系统整理
- Ecology case notes 生态案例笔记
- Review questions and concept links 复习问答与概念联系

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

Life rarely shouts its order; it grows it slowly in silence.

生命很少高声宣布自己的秩序，它更常在沉默中缓慢生长。

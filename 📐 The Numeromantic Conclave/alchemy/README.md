# alchemy | 化学

This is the hall where matter confesses what it is made of, and what it may yet become.

这里是物质坦白自身组成，并展示其仍可能变成何物的分殿。

---

## Hall Charge | 学科之责

Alchemy studies composition, structure, reaction, transformation, and the logic of substances. It trains the eye to see hidden change beneath ordinary appearance.

化学之学研究组成、结构、反应、转化与物质背后的规则。它训练人看见日常外表之下正在发生的微观变化。

## What Is Studied Here | 研习内容

- Atomic structure and periodicity 原子结构与元素周期性
- Chemical bonds and molecular form 化学键与分子结构
- Reactions, equations, and stoichiometry 反应、方程式与化学计量
- Solutions, acids, bases, and laboratory reasoning 溶液、酸碱与实验思维

## Suggested Holdings | 建议收录

- Reaction summaries 反应规律整理
- Equation practice 方程式练习
- Lab notes 实验记录
- Concept maps 概念图
- Error review sheets 错误归纳

## Hall Saying | 分殿短谚

Transformation is not chaos; it is law passing through fire.

变化并非混乱，而是法则穿过火焰时的形状。
